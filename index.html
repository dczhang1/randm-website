About Our Lab

The Risk and Decision Making Lab is dedicated to understanding how individuals and groups navigate the complexities of decision-making under uncertainty in the workplace. Our interdisciplinary team brings together experts in psychology, management, and data science.

We conduct cutting-edge research that bridges theory and practice, tackling questions like: How do employees weigh risks in high-stakes decisions? What influences risk perception at work? How can organizations create environments that support effective decision making?

Our mission is to advance scientific knowledge and provide insights that inform workplace policies and management practices that promote better decision-making outcomes.

Research Focus Areas

Risk Perception at Work

Investigating how employees perceive and evaluate risks across different organizational contexts and situations.

Decision-Making Under Uncertainty

Examining the cognitive processes and factors that influence workplace decisions when outcomes are uncertain.

Individual Differences in Risk-Taking

Exploring how personality traits and other individual characteristics affect willingness to take risks in professional settings.
