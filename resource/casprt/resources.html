Data Repository

All study data, materials, and code are available on the Open Science Framework:

Access the OSF Repository

The repository includes:

  • Raw data from all seven studies
  • Complete survey materials
  • Analysis code (R scripts)
  • Supplemental analyses and figures
  • Preregistrations for confirmatory studies

Using the CASPRT in Research or Practice

The CASPRT is freely available for use in research and practice. We ask that you:

  1. Cite the original publication when using the scale
  2. Do not modify the items without permission from the authors
  3. Share your findings with us to help improve the scale and expand its applications

For permissions beyond standard research use, collaborative opportunities, or to discuss the scale's application in specific contexts, please contact the authors.
