Our Philosophy

Uncertainty is not the enemy of decision-making; it is its raw material.

The Risk & Decision Making Lab investigates the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, and behavioral economics. We utilize advanced fMRI techniques, computational modeling, and immersive virtual reality environments to understand how humans navigate ambiguous environments. From financial trading to clinical diagnosis, our research reveals the hidden variables driving choice.
