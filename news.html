March 27, 2024

Don Zhang receives the LSU Rainmaker Award

Lab director Don Zhang receives the LSU Rainmaker Award, recognizing outstanding research, scholarship, and creative activity.

Lab director Don Zhang receives the LSU Rainmaker Award, which is given each year to faculty who show outstanding research, scholarship, and creative activity for their respective ranks and discipline.

The award ceremony was held at the LSU Faculty Club, where Dr. Zhang was recognized among other distinguished faculty members for his contributions to the field of risk and decision-making research.
