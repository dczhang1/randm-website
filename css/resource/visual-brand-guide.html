Typography

Our typography system uses two complementary fonts - Lora for headings and Poppins for body text.

Lora (for headings)

Document Title (Lora, 18-24pt)

Section Heading (Lora, 14-16pt)

Substitute with Georgia or Times New Roman if Lora is unavailable.

Poppins (for body text)

Subheading (Poppins Semibold, 12-14pt)

Body text (Poppins Regular, 11-12pt). This is an example of body text that would be used in documents, presentations, and other materials. The Poppins font provides excellent readability even at smaller sizes.

Captions/Citations (Poppins, 9-10pt). Used for figure captions, footnotes, and citations.

Substitute with Arial or Helvetica if Poppins is unavailable.

Document Applications

Guidelines for applying these design elements to common document types.

Presentations
  • Use white or Light Beige backgrounds
  • Headings in Deep Navy Blue
  • Accents and highlights in Muted Brown
  • Keep slides clean with adequate white space
  • Use Lora for slide titles, Poppins for content
Posters
  • Use white background for maximum readability
  • Section titles in Lora, Deep Navy Blue
  • Body text in Poppins
  • Use Medium Blue for chart elements
  • Include RANDM logo in upper left corner
Reports and Papers
  • Titles and headings in Lora
  • Body text in Poppins
  • Use Medium Blue for callout boxes
  • Figures and tables with consistent styling
  • Citations in Poppins, 10pt
